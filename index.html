About Me

Technical depth.
Customer empathy.

After 12 years as a Database Administrator & Cloud Engineer, I have moved into a Customer Success role at EnterpriseDB as a Senior Technical Account Manager — leveraging deep expertise in PostgreSQL, Oracle, and cloud infrastructure to deliver impactful solutions for complex customer challenges.

I provide proactive technical guidance on architecture, performance optimisation, security, and best practices — leading technical reviews, health checks, and assessments to ensure optimal database performance and reliability. I support customers through migrations, upgrades, and critical implementations.

I specialise in RDBMS technologies including Oracle, PostgreSQL, MySQL, and MSSQL Server, managing their lifecycle across on-premises and public cloud environments. My expertise spans backup & recovery, high availability, disaster recovery, performance tuning, database security, and DBaaS solutions including AWS RDS and Aurora.

As a Cloud Engineer, I have extensive experience across public cloud services — compute, storage, networking, security, IAM, and compliance. I have also worked extensively with NoSQL databases, data warehousing (IBM Netezza, Snowflake), and event streaming with Apache Kafka.
